About me

I am a postdoc in mathematical image processing, inverse problems and machine learning within the research group "applied mathematics" at TU Berlin. Here, I completed my PhD in March 2023 supervised by Gabriele Steidl. I obtained my BSc and MSc degrees in Mathematics in 2018 and 2020 from TU Kaiserslautern (renamed in 2023 to RPTU). During my BSc and MSc I was supported by a scholarship of the Felix Klein Center for Mathematics.

My brother Christoph works in combinatorial optimization.
